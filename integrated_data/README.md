## Integrated Data Directory

This directory contains 1 file: `integrated.csv`, which was generated from `/scripts/integrate_data.py`. Here is metadata for the file:

`name`: The Name of the Core-Based Statistical Area (CBSA)

`cbsa_code`: The unique 5-digit code for the CBSA

`median_income`: The median household income, in 2024 inflation-adjusted U.S. Dollars

`hs_diploma_per_greater_than_25_pop`: The proportion of the population 25+ that has a high school diploma

`ged_per_greater_than_25_pop`: The proportion of the population aged 25+ that has a GED or alternative credential

`associates_per_greater_than_25_pop`: The proportion of the population aged 25+ that has an Associate's Degree

`bachelors_per_greater_than_25_pop`: The proportion of the population aged 25+ that has a Bachelor's Degree

`masters_per_greater_than_25_pop`: The proportion of the population aged 25+ that has a Master's Degree

`professional_per_greater_than_25_pop`: The proportion of the population aged 25+ that has a Professional School Degree

`doctorate_per_greater_than_25_pop`: The proportion of the population aged 25+ that has a Doctorate Degree

`Public_Schools_per_total_pop`: The number of public schools in a CBSA divided by the total population in that CBSA

`Postsecondary_Schools_per_total_pop`: The number of postsecondary schools in a CBSA divided by the total population in that CBSA
